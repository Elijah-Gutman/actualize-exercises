How I got here

To tell a very long story within a few short words I spent most of my twenties in political advococy and fundraising. Like anyone with a bit of emotional maturity could tell you, politics is a souless vapid means of wealth accumulation. Its enriching oneself at the cost of everyone else. Abbrivated: I now seek to actually add value and productivity to this world. The best way I see to do that is by using my propensity and proclivity for problem solving as a means to do so. Enter a desire to become and software engineer. It also helps that everyone in my immidiate circle are already in tech.
